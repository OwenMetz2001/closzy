Who, What, Why is CLOSZY?

Sanne


Fleur

Owen

In a world where danger lies in the dim-lit alleyways, the empty parking lots, and even our warm homes, we believe that everyone deserves to move freely without fear, because only then one will be able to actually live to their fullest. CLOSZY is there for those who suffer feelings of insecurity. For those who are scared to walk alone at night, or even during the day. CLOSZY was established by and for women (and Owen), to help our own out. To be a guiding hand through the scary dark streets, to bring light and warmth and safety back into your homes, back into your lives. Join us on our journey to a world with the same level of comfort of the blanket forts you used to build as a kid.

Our Mission

CLOSZY recognizes the harsh reality of the world we live in. It is simply not safe for all women to be out and about on the streets. However, we want to spread awareness of the issue, whilst also empowering women and handing them tools to defend themselves against attackers. We all want to live a good life, let's work towards that, together.
