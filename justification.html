User Testing Report

We tested our digital wirefram prototype among ten participants. They were gives full access to the XD wireframe on a laptop.
The results showed:

  • The website looks professional and structured
  • Structured and nice layout
  • Add options like a chat, a wish list or create an account
  • Write a more detailed description of the products
  • Make the NavBar more legible
  • Add a "jump to top button"
  • Make the shopping cart a seperate page
  • More information about the team behind CLOSZY
  • Reduce the amount of white space

From the user tests, we concluded that new and more detailed pages should be created for the products. There should be more focus on the content in the "About" section, and the NavBar should be better legible. On top of this, the layout could be enhanced by reduces the whiite space, and finally there should be a "jump to top" button to enhance user-experience.
