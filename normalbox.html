Normal Box


The normal box brings you an array of products to enhance your safety as well as your feeling of empowerment. It comes with four different items which vary each month.
Each box contains:

  • One self defence tool, for example pepper spray or a flashlight.
  • The monthly CLOSZY magazine with self defence tips, empowering and inspring stories of other women, ways to bring more awareness to the problem and more.
  • A card with an inpiring quote.
  • A snack
